## Origin

Fruckenstein is the public packaging of the relational duck work directed by James Sterling Tuttle (“V”) for Alpha Data Omega in Houston, Texas.

The organizing human contributions include:

- persistent relational consequence rather than a direct memory-to-joint command;
- refusal as a valid controller output;
- the crown/seat geometry and its role as a slow commitment layer;
- the relation between voxel perception and voxel reality;
- whole-gait experience played through the body;
- forward/left/right sign correction from observed motion;
- appetite, nociception, vitality, death, babble, and physical flock contact;
- morphology-bound inheritance and rebaking after body changes;
- gated generational development and retention of a working gait.

AI coding systems assisted as tools with repository research, mathematical formalization, software implementation, debugging, simulation, packaging, tests, and documentation. No AI system is asserted as an inventor, authorizing person, owner, or conscious participant.
